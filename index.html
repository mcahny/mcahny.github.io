Electrical Engineering, KAIST

    Electrical Engineering, KAIST

I am a Ph.D. student advised by Professor In So Kweon, at Electrical Engineering, KAIST. I interned at Adobe(san Jose, CA) with Joon-Young Lee in 2019, and will be interning at Google Brain for Summer 2020. I am a recepient of Microsoft Research Asia Fellowship (top 12 students in Asia-Pacific) and Global Ph.D Fellowship from NRF Korea.

My research interests lie in machine learning and computer vision. Particularly, my goal is to create scalable deep learning models that can understand visual data with minimal human supervision. Research topics I have studied include recognition, image/video understanding (high level, pixel level), self-supervised learning and weakly-supervised learning.
